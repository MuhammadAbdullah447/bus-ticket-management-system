Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with BusConnect?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact our sales team, and we'll set up a demo account for you to explore the platform. Once you're ready to proceed, we'll help you with the full implementation.

Is technical support included?

Yes, all BusConnect plans include technical support. Our standard support hours are Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm. Premium support packages with 24/7 assistance are also available.

Can BusConnect integrate with my existing systems?

BusConnect offers various integration options with popular accounting, CRM, and payment processing systems. Our team can work with you to determine the best integration strategy for your specific needs.

Is training provided for our staff?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training for your staff as part of the implementation process. Additional training sessions can be arranged as needed for new employees or refresher courses.
